ABOUT US

What is ePainting?

ePainting is a one-of-a-kind online art gallery where buyers could buy famous paintings painted by the most renowned artists and painters worldwide. Our team collects all kinds of paintings starting from the Renaissance period until the modern age. In the new millennium, painting has recovered its popularity, and the practice has remained fairly unchanged. Paints remain a mixture of colored pigment and binder, and common subject matters still comprise imagery or inspiration from the objects or events in artists' daily lives. The contemporary painter's palette consists not only of colors, but the range of historic painting styles.

What do we do?

Paintings both reflect the artist's point-of-view and possess an independent voice which speaks a unique message to each beholder. Some collectors focus on media, such as oil paintings on canvas, or watercolors. Others specialize in a particular style, be it fine art, street art, or expressionist paintings.

As the world's largest online art gallery, ePainting is thrilled to help people everywhere discover, fall in love with, and own one-of-a-kind paintings by some of the most talented emerging artists from around the world.

What do we offer?

We offer:
Global Selection; we have an unparalleled selection of paintings, photography, sculpture, and drawings by over 50,000 artists from around the world.
Satisfaction Guaranteed; our 7-day 100% money-back guarantee allows you to buy with confidence. If for any reason you're not satisfied with your purchase, return it and we'll help you find a work you love.
Original Art for Any Budget; ePainting features works at a wide range of prices to suit all budgets and projects.
Global Shipping; we handle all aspects of international shipping and customs for a completely hassle-free delivery.

What are we looking for?

We believe in paying artists the maximum possible amount in order to have a healthy, sustainable relationship. We pay the highest earnings in the industry. Artists receive 70% of the sale price for original works and 70% of net profit for ePainting prints. One of ePainting's goals is to help artists sell art. We understand artists have a number of options, so we've made ePainting's Sales Agreement non-exclusive.

All we ask is for you to keep your artwork up-to-date and de-list the art for sale if it becomes unavailable.

Who are we?

We, the collectors, offer an unparalleled selection of paintings, drawings, sculpture and photography in a range of prices, and it provides artists from around the world with an expertly curated environment in which to exhibit and sell their work.

Based in Chicago, ePainting is redefining the experience of buying and selling art by making it easy, convenient and welcoming for both collectors and artists.
